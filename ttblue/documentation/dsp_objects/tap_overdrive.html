tap_overdrive
Soft saturation
Distorts signals by applying a nonlinear transfer function (wave-shaping), thereby simulating an overdriven amplifier.
Attributes
    k_drive If drive is 1., the signal is unchanged. Increasing drive increases the distortion. [1.0 - 10.0, default: 3.0].
