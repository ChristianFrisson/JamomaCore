tap_degrade
Lo-fi effects
Distorts signals by reducing the sample-rate and/or bit depth.
Attributes
    k_bitdepth The bit depth of the audio. May be an integer between 1 and 24.
    k_sr_ratio The ratio of the audio's sample-rate to the object's sample rate. Should be in the range of 0.0 to 1.0.
