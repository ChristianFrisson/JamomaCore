tap_lowpass_fourpole
Resonant four-pole lowpass filter
Attributes
    k_frequency The cutoff frequency of the filter specified in Hertz [0.0 - Nyquist, default: 4000 Hz].
    k_resonance The amount of resonance for the filter [0.0 - 10.0, default: 4.0].
Methods
    clear() Use to reset the buffers other feedback storage. Handy in the event of something blowing-up.
