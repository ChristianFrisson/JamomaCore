tap_crossfade
Crossfades between two signals, mixing them down to one
Attributes
    k_shape The mathematical shape of the crossfade (linear or equal-power). The attribute should be set with one of the following selectors:
      k_shape_equalpower [default]
      k_shape_linear
    k_mode The method of calculation for the crossfade (lookup-table or on-the-fly calculation). The attribute should be set with one of the following selectors:
      k_mode_lookup [default]
      k_mode_calculate
    k_position The position of the crossfade between the left input[0.0] and the right input[1.0].
